Our System

Challenges

We believe that a core aspect of breaking adddictions is through accountability. This allows the addicted user to know that someone is watching their progress, and is also there to support them. Thus, the user is more likely to put in greater effort in breaking the addiction. Through our challenges system, users can create a challenge that helps them tackle their addiction. Other users can then join this challenge, and both users are now accountable for each other and are there for moral support.

Community Blog

When tackling addictions, it is very important to be aware of your progress. There are a lot of different emotions that we experience when overcoming emotions, and it is important to realize that these are simply emotions them selves. By writing about these topics, it will not only allow users to clear their mind, yet also allow other users to provide their own advice.

Community Chat

Sometimes, emergencies come up and the urge to relapse becomes very strong. In times like these, a challenge or blog post may not be motivating enough to keep going. For situations like these, on-demand support is very helpful. Therefore we have our chat system that allows you to talk to someone right away, and explain your emergency.
